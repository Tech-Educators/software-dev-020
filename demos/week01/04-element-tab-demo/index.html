Spaghetti-Eating Squirrel Shocks Suburbs

Residents Left Confused and Amused

Mas Sidouloul

Yesterday, an unusually daring squirrel was spotted sitting on a park bench devouring an entire plate of spaghetti. Witnesses reported seeing it calmly twirl noodles with its tiny paws, savoring every bite. A local resident called the phenomenon “bizarre yet oddly entertaining.”

Experts speculate that the squirrel might have rummaged through a picnic basket or trash bin to score its carb-loaded meal. Animal enthusiasts suggest the furry diner exhibited remarkable dexterity. Curious onlookers took photos and videos, posting them online with captions like #SpaghettiSquirrel.

The city council has not released an official statement, but an environmental group hints that a new initiative might be in the works to better manage local wildlife feeding behaviors.
